my season ambition

SMART goal

The framework I used to set a clear, reachable target for my pitching this season.

S

Specific

I will improve my pitching mechanics by fixing my knuckle rotation and increasing my leg drive off the rubber.

M

Measurable

I will track and improve my overall strike percentage throughout the season.

A

Achievable

Four months of focused practice is enough time to gain more consistent strikes.

R

Relevant

Throwing more strikes will help my team win and make me a more reliable pitcher.

T

Time-bound

I will reach my target strike percentage by the end of four months.

putting it all together

Over the next four months, I will improve my knuckle rotation and leg drive to raise my strike percentage from [current]% to [goal]% by practicing three times a week — making me a more effective pitcher.
